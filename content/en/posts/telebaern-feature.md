On March 11, 2022, TeleBärn broadcast a segment about bidirectional charging stations.

sun2wheel and TCS appeared together to present the innovative technology of bidirectional charging. The segment explained how electric vehicles can not only draw electricity but also feed it back into the home network or the power grid.

## Topics covered:

- What is bidirectional charging?
- How does Vehicle-to-Home (V2H) work?
- What advantages does the technology offer for homeowners?
- The partnership between sun2wheel and TCS

The television feature reached a broad audience and helped raise awareness of the possibilities of bidirectional charging technology in Switzerland.
